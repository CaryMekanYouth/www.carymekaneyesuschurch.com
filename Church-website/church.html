Welcome to Ethiopian Evangelical Cary Mekane Yesus Church

Here at Cary Mekane Yesus church, our main goal is to have a Christ-like mindset and putting others and God first. Whether you're from Ethiopia, America, or anywhere else, we invite you to worship with us and experience the love of Christ as a community, where the service is held in both English and Amharic. We come together every Sunday to worship God as one congregation and read his word. May God Bless you, brothers and sisters in Christ!

What to Expect

In the beginning of the service, we start off with an opening prayer and scripture reading. After that we go into our worship songs led by a different members of the church every week. Next, all the kids from ages 2-18 go up to the front for a short lesson before Sunday School. Then the adults would have their sermon. At the end of all church services, there is time for everyone to socialize a bit before everyone goes so feel free to enjoy with all the other members!

Every Sunday 4:00-6:30! Feel free to come early to settle in and talk to friends
