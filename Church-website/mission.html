How it all started

When the church announced that they had began to raise money for the church, the youth group came up with the idea to create a GoFundMe and a website to promote it so that people from anywhere in the country could pitch and help the church raise money. We created different social media accounts such as Facebook and YouTube so that people could learn more about us and our goal toward having our own church. We hope that you would be willing to donate any amount on the GoFundMe. Even small donations can make a big difference. Any donation is welcome.
