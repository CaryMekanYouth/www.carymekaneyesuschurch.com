Our Mission:

Our mission is mostly focused around Matthew 28:19-20, which states, "Go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit." We want people to know more about the Lord and to accept Jesus as their Savior. Those who are willing to be a part of Christ's famliy is welcome to come into our church to read God's Word and worship with us.

Our Vision:

Our vision is for our church to grow in its faith, having more people who are willing to join us and worship the Lord, and faithfully innovating while sharing the love of Christ through service from the heart and the proclamation of the Gospel. We hope to expand and evangilize to those who haven't heard God's Word so that they may be changed for the better and be a part of God's family.
